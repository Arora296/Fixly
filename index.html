Our story
Built for the people who build

Fixly was born out of a simple frustration: finding a reliable, skilled professional in India shouldn't be this hard. Whether it's a leaking pipe, a faulty switch, or a complete home renovation — getting the right person for the job has always felt like a lottery.

We set out to change that. Fixly is a platform that bridges the gap between talented tradespeople and the homeowners, renters, and businesses who need them — building trust on both sides through transparency, accountability, and great work.

We are proudly based in India, built for Indian homes, and focused on the unique challenges of our local market. From metro apartments to smaller-town houses, Fixly brings professional-quality service to your doorstep.

Every professional on our platform is personally vetted, trained to communicate clearly, and held to a consistent quality standard. If a job doesn't meet expectations, we make it right — no arguments, no hassle.

We believe that the people who fix, build, and maintain our homes deserve just as much respect and opportunity as anyone else. Fixly gives skilled workers a professional platform to grow their business, find steady work, and be recognised for the quality they deliver.

Aarav Arora

Founder & CEO, Fixly

Aarav Arora started Fixly after experiencing firsthand how difficult it was to find trustworthy home-service professionals in India. A firm believer in the dignity of skilled labour, he built Fixly to empower workers and give clients a service they could truly rely on.

His vision is straightforward: a platform where quality work is rewarded, where workers are respected, and where every homeowner can find the right help without stress.
